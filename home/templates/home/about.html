About Kicassoo

Starting in the heart of East London, Kicassoo's artistic journey is deeply rooted in the rich cultural tapestry and dynamic street art of Brick Lane and Shoreditch. These vibrant areas, renowned for their expressive and quirky street art, have profoundly influenced her creative vision. Music, culture, and modern societal traditions are the lifeblood of her art, infusing each piece with a rhythm and vibrancy that speak to the soul. Whether it's the beats of a song or the colors of her cultural heritage, these elements come together in a harmonious blend on her canvases.

Since moving to Southend, Kicassoo has embraced a new wave of creativity, engaging with the community through various artistic initiatives. Over the past three years, she has brought art to the public through pop-up events, arts and crafts sessions, and interactive group activities like Sip and Paint. Her commitment to fostering creativity in others is evident in her dedication to one-on-one sessions and community workshops.

Kicassoo is an abstract artist at heart, primarily working with acrylic paints but never limiting herself to one medium. She believes in the power of art to transcend form and function, offering viewers a moment to pause, reflect, and connect with a memory or emotion captured in time. Each piece is a journey into a still moment, inviting you to explore and feel the depth of her artistic expression.

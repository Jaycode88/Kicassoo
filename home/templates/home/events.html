This is Us; The Beauty Behind the Brush

An Exhibition by Kicassoo

Date: 02.12.2024 - 31.12.2024

Location: The People's Gallery, The Forum, Elmer Square, Southend-on-Sea, Essex, SS1 1NS

Join us for a captivating journey through the artistic evolution of Kicassoo, a talented abstract artist known for her vibrant and emotive works. This December, immerse yourself in her latest exhibition, "This is Us: The Beauty Behind the Brush," at The People's Gallery in Southend-on-Sea.

Kicassoo's exhibition displays a loving and truthful story of her journey in art. From tears to cheers, and black and white to bouncing bright colors, her work captures the essence of a person's emotions at a specific time and place. From her happiest moments to her deepest thoughts, Kicassoo's art speaks volumes.

Some of her greatest works come from the Animal Kingdom collection, showcasing her passion and dedication. This exhibition is a testament to her hard work and artistic development over the past few years. Stay updated on more about this exhibition on Instagram:
